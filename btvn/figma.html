Course Description

Do you want to become a UI/UX designer but you don’t know where to start? This course will
allow you to develop your user interface design skills and you can add UI designer to your CV 
and start getting clients for your skills.

Hi everyone. I’m Arash and I’m a UI/UX designer. In this course, I will help you learn and
master Figma app comprehensively from scratch. Figma is an innovative and brilliant tool for User Interface design.
It’s used by everyone from entrepreneurs and start-ups to Apple, Airbnb, Facebook, etc.
